ABOUT

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a system where the users of this website can succeed in making small changes in the lives of the people in need. When a system is created where people are having a medium to donate to a helpful cause, it will be easier for them as well as useful for everyone as a whole.

The Process

The process is fairly simple. You can choose to donate either or all of these three things: food, clothes and medication for various ailments. A truck will sent to your location after we get in contact with you. The rest of the process will be done by us. All of the materials collected will be provided to the ones in need on a specific day and location that will be announced in the website.
